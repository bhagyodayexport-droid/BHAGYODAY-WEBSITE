Why ROOFY

Not Just a Sheet. An Engineered System.

Three reasons ROOFY outperforms every traditional roofing system in India's harshest conditions.

🔩 roofy-installation.jpg — 600×338px
🚫
FEATURE 01
Zero-Hole. Zero-Leak Architecture.
No screws drilled through the sheet. No lap joints. No exposed fasteners anywhere on the roof. The robotic Zip-Up tool crimps every panel into a 180°–360° mechanical lock — the roof physically seals itself shut.
NO HOLES = NO LEAKS. EVER.

🤖
FEATURE 02
Robotic Precision. Not Human Guesswork.
Every seam is machine-crimped uniformly from one end of the roof to the other. No weak points, no inconsistent hand-tightening, no rework. On-site roll-forming means panels up to 120 Mtr+ with zero transport damage or mid-span joints.
360° MACHINE LOCKED SEAM

FEATURE 03
Solar Panel Ready. No Drilling Required.
Solar mounting brackets clamp directly onto the standing seam — zero drilling, zero roof penetration. Future-proof your building without ever creating a new leak point. ROOFY is built for India's solar revolution.
